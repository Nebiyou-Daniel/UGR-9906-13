FAQ

What does the company make?

The company produces two electrical appliances namely Mitad and Stoves. The former is used to make Ethiopia's native food Injera while the latter is a traditional tool for cooking.


What makes our company's product different from other similar products?

The products made by our company has been proven to be the most efficient of its kind for more than the past five years or so. It has received countless amount of recognition over the years.


How are our products efficient?

Our product's efficiency comes from careful study of day-to-day appliances, discovering their weaknesses and providing an alternative solution. They are made using domestic resources and can potentially save one household as much as half their average electricity consumption cost.


Why hasn't the company expanded its business potential all across Ethiopia?

This is a simple case of lack of funding and investments from investors and the government.
