Daniel Asrat

Mr. Daniel Asrat is the firm owner who has the educational qualification and more than 37 years of experience in Electrical installation & design, IT, project management and energy efficiency projects. He has been awarded a minor Invention certificate on efficiency improvement of Injera mitad set from Ethiopian Intellectual property office, Ministry of Science and Technology, Ethiopia. Based on the research and study made, the energy efficiency of electric injera Mitad has been improved to 81%, for the first time in Ethiopia. The energy efficiency of the electric Mitad has been tested and certificate was issued by Ministry of water and electricity, Directorate of alternative energy technologies development
