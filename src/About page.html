DANAS Electrical Engineering

DANAS Electrical Engineering, established in 2003, is engaged in electrical engineering consultancy services and manufacturing of energy efficient electric injera Mitad and electric stoves in Ethiopia. Danas Electrical Engineering has accomplished several project studies, Electrical design and instillation works for private and government institutions and energy efficiency works. To improve the energy efficiency of locally produced injera and cooking stoves, Danas electrical has developed stoves having higher energy efficiency through minimizing heat losses exhibited on the products in the market. The energy efficient Electric Injera Mitad and Stoves are on sale at the workshop of DANAS Electrical Engineering.

Some of consultancy works carried out include:

  1. Assessment for the Implementation of Solar Water Heaters (SWH) to Hotels, Hospitals and Other Commercial Buildings in Addis Ababa, completed in January 2022.
  2. Efficiency Assessment and Minimum Efficiency Performance Standard (MEPS) setting for Induction motors in Ethiopian Market, completed in June 2021.
  3. Development of Energy efficient locally manufactured electric stove prototype and training of same to Ethiopian Producers in the Nine regional states and administrative cities. May 2019 - to date. Product has been tested by Min of Water, irrigation, and Electricity in 2020.
  4. Prepared project document on Energy efficiency standards and labeling for Locally manufactured Electric stoves in Ethiopia, Ethiopian Energy Authority. December 2016 - February 2017.
  5. Prepared project document on Energy efficiency standards and labeling for Electric motors in Ethiopia, Ethiopian Energy Authority. April. 2015 – June. 2015.
  6. Produced project document on Energy efficiency and standards labeling for Electric Injera Mitad. Nov. 2014 – Jan. 2015.
  7. Produced study report on the Improvement of Electrical Injera baking pan (Mitad). The study report has been submitted to Ethiopian Energy Authority for the Energy Efficiency award for the Energy Efficiency award competition in August 2014. The study has been selected as the top Energy Efficiency studies in the country and award received.
  8. Electrical Design of African Union Old buildings lighting, power, fire alarm and computer Data networks. AE Consult, 0911200648
  9. Electrical Design and Supervision of African Union Conference Hall Project AE Consult, 0911200648
  10. Electrical Design, Tender document Preparation and bid evaluation for Ethiopian Air farce Debrezeit Airbase Airfield lighting as a sub contract to the government owned Transport Construction Design Enterprise, Addis Ababa.
  11. Electrical Design for the rehabilitation Addis Ababa block production Enterprise
  12. Arbaminch Airport and Airfield site selection study and Electrical design for Airport Terminal building.
  13. Jimma Airport Terminal building Electrical design.
  14. Electrical Installation of Sets General trading plc Aluminum and metal work shop and office.
